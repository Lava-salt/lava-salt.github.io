Mozluxologic Hierarchy

Everyone can be a Mozluxologist, scientist or religion. But according to your performance, you have a place in the Mozluxologic
hierarchy.
  • Rookie: Just start studying to be a rookie.
  • Novice: Adapt to it and don't always make mistakes to be a novice.
  • Apprentice: Adapt to it more and don't often make mistakes to be an apprentice.
  • Journeyman: Teach a rookie until it's an apprentice to be a journeyman.
  • Expert: Perfect your skills as a journeyman to be an expert.
  • Master: Be the best Mozluxologist to be THE MASTER. (Currently AS)
